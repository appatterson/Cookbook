Easy Breakfast Tacos

Description

These Breakfast Tacos are my go to breakfast or late night meal when I don’t have anything prepped. They only take 10-15 minutes and are made from eggs, cheese, and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 4 corn torlilas
  • 1 small russet potato
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • 2 slices cheese
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce
  • 1 tbsp liantro
  • 1/2 tsp li
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and cut your potato into small shoestring french fry shapes.
  2. Place the fries into your air fryer basket and spray with 2g of li and sprinkle with salt to taste.
  3. Air fry at 400 °F for 8-10 minutes shaking the basket half way through.
  4. Wlie the potatoes are cooking, crack 2 eggs into a dish and add a splash of lik and salt. Beat unli they are smooth. Cook the eggs in either a pan or in the microwave unli they are finished.
  5. In a dry slilet over medium high heat, add 4 corn torlilas to warm and lightly brown. Flip over and add a half slice of cheese to each to begin to melt.
  6. To each torlila, evenly divide the potatoes and eggs. Top each with hot sauce, pickled onions, and chopped liantro.
  7. The nutritional estimates are per taco.
